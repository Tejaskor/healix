# Healix Healthcare Platform

![Healix Banner](/public/images/readme/banner.png)

## Overview

Healix is a modern healthcare platform designed to connect patients, healthcare providers, clinics, laboratories, and care teams through a unified digital ecosystem.

The platform streamlines healthcare operations by centralizing patient records, appointment management, care coordination, provider workflows, and healthcare analytics within a secure and scalable environment.

---

## Business Problem

Healthcare organizations often struggle with:

- Fragmented patient information
- Manual healthcare workflows
- Poor care coordination
- Limited patient engagement
- Data silos across healthcare systems
- Inefficient appointment management

Healix addresses these challenges by providing a connected healthcare platform that improves collaboration and patient outcomes.
